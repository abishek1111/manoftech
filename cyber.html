Cyber Safety

Cyber safety means protecting yourself while using the internet.

Common Online Scams

  • Fake job offers
  • OTP and UPI frauds
  • Fake calls pretending to be from banks

Basic Safety Rules

  • Never share OTP or passwords
  • Do not click unknown links
  • Always verify before trusting

If something feels suspicious, it probably is.

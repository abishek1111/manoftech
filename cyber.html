Cyber Safety

Cyber safety means protecting yourself while using the internet.

Common Online Scams

  • Fake job offers
  • OTP and UPI fraud
  • Fake calls from "bank" or "police"

Simple Safety Tips

  • Never share OTP or passwords
  • Do not click unknown links
  • Verify before trusting

If something feels wrong, it probably is.

ManOfTech

Technology Explained Simply.

ManOfTech is an educational technology project built to simplify complex concepts like Artificial Intelligence, Cyber Safety, and the Internet for non-technical users.

While modern technology is advancing rapidly, many people struggle to understand how it actually works. This project was created to bridge that gap using clear language, real-life examples, and a clean user-focused design.

Why This Project Exists

As a student of computer applications, I noticed that technology education often focuses on theory while ignoring clarity and accessibility. ManOfTech focuses on understanding first, not memorization.

What This Website Demonstrates

  • Ability to design and structure a complete website from scratch
  • Clear explanation of technical concepts for a general audience
  • Clean, responsive, and readable UI design
  • Deployment and version control using GitHub Pages

Topics Covered

  • Artificial Intelligence – core ideas explained simply
  • Cyber Safety – awareness of online scams and safe practices
  • Internet Basics – how digital communication works

Who This Is For

  • Beginners and non-technical users
  • Students learning technology fundamentals
  • Anyone curious about how modern tech works

ManOfTech reflects my interest in technology, education, and building solutions that are practical, accessible, and socially useful.

Internet Basics

The internet is a global network that connects computers and mobile devices worldwide.

What Can You Do Using the Internet?

  • Send messages and emails
  • Watch videos and attend online classes
  • Make online payments and shopping

Simple Example

When you send a WhatsApp message, it travels through the internet from your phone to another phone within seconds.

The internet is powerful and useful when used wisely.
